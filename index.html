About Alice

A midwest woman through and through, Alice brings a blend of observational and satirical comedy. She is yet another person to fall victim to the theatre to comedy pipeline. Her clever wit and bold storytelling makes her not one to miss.

Alice has performed at venues across the country including Mark Ridley's Comedy Castle (MI), Ann Arbor Comedy Showcase (MI), The Comedy Fort (CO), Comedy at the Carlson(NY), Comedy Works (CO), The Independent Comedy Club (MI), and Detroit House of Comedy (MI) to name a few.

Alice was highlighted as Comic of the Week on Jackie Kashian's podcast, The Jackie and Laurie Show. When not on stage you can find her browsing the Costco aisles or keeping her roots alive in dance class.
